PlatformCostWebsite BuilderDescription
GoDaddy$9.99-20.99YesA hosting platform that allows users to upload their own files or use their website builder to create a site.

WixFree with paid optionsYesWeb hosting with more advanced drag and drop tools, allowing even more customization for those who prefer being able to manipulate the smallest details.
Google SitesFreeYesA simple, template based website builder provided for free through the suite of Google Applications.
WordpressFree but minimal functionalityYesA mostly text based platform with a variety of templates to choose from. The free version has minimal functionality, but there are paid options to increase your options.
SquarespaceFree with paid optionsYesMostly advertised for ecommerce, but it's also a simple template based platform that has drag and drop features to easily add images, videos, and text to your website.
