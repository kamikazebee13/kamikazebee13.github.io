An Interactive Short Story

A short interactive story from the perspective of an insomniac. Use the buttons below the text to make your choices to advance the story. Refresh the page to start over at any point. Have fun!

This is an interactive short story from the perspective of an insomniac. While none of the events in this short story are real, they are based off of my own experiences with insomnia. Sometimes just playing or reading something calming like this has been enough to help put me to sleep. I don't know if this will help anyone else, but my fingers are crossed for you my insomniac friends.
